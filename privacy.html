Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to Pushup Debt. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal information when you use our iOS application.

1. Data Collection & On-Device Processing

Pushup Debt is designed with your privacy in mind. We generally strive to minimize the data we collect. Core features require certain permissions:

  • Camera: Used locally on your device for tracking push-ups. Video data is processed on-device in real-time and is not saved, recorded, or transmitted to any external servers.
  • HealthKit: Used to sync your fitness data. Health data remains on your device or in your personal iCloud account. We do not sell or use your health data for advertising purposes.
  • Location: Handled locally to verify anti-cheat mechanisms.

2. Analytics

We use third-party analytics services (such as PostHog) to understand how the app is used, track bugs, and improve the user experience. This data is anonymized and does not include your personal health or camera view data.

3. Cloud Services & Subscriptions

To provide cross-device support, leaderboards, and manage premium subscriptions, we may securely store basic account, workout statistics, and subscription statuses using our backend infrastructure. Sensitive data like passwords are encrypted.

4. Children's Privacy

Pushup Debt is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Support Page.
